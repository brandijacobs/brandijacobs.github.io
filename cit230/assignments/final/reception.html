Reception Accommodations

We want your wedding to be as prefect as possible. To do this, we made our prices budget friendly, and we organized our hotel around your convinence. Wedding couple hotel rooms are located just down the hall from the reception hall, and there is a bridal suite and kitchen attached to the hall. If any more accommodations need to be made, please specify in the "Accommodations / Comments" section of your reservation.

Here at Temple Inn & Suites, we have a great realationship with the local businesses around the area. If a resturant offers catering, we can set that up for you free of charge! All you have to do is leave the name of the resturant in the special accommodations / comments section of your reservation. If the resturant doesn't cater, we offer catering of their food for $100 more, as long as the resturant is within a 5 mile radius of the hotel.

Setup

As stated, we offer a 150 sq ft bridal suite and a 450 sq ft kitchen attached at the north end of the reception hall.

If you chose us to cater, we provide 2 employees to set up an hour before your reception, have the food ready 15 minutes before, serve the food during, and clean up promptly after.

Included in the contract price are all the tables (up to 25 6ft circle & 20 5ft rectangle) with white linens provided. These will be washed and pressed before your event.
