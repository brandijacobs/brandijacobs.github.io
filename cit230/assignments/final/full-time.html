Temple Missionary Suites

We believe worrying about where to live while serving the Lord shouldn't be an issue. That's why we designed rooms to be comfortable, nice, and affordable. We offer two rooms for our temple missionaries: 1 King Bed Suite & 1 Queen Bed Suite. All our full-time temple missionary suites are on the first level and are handicap rooms.
